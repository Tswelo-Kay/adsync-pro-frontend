Frequently Asked Questions
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period with no extra charges.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit/debit cards, and we use Paystack for secure South African payments including local bank cards.
Do you offer a free trial on paid plans? ▼
The Free plan is yours forever with no credit card required. We're also offering 3 months free on Pro for waitlist members at launch.
Is my store data safe? ▼
Absolutely. Your API keys and store data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties and you can delete your account at any time.
