01

Information We Collect

When you use AdSync Pro, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (encrypted) when you register
  • Business information — your store name, product listings, and ad campaign data
  • Platform tokens — OAuth access tokens from Meta, Shopify, and other platforms you connect (stored encrypted)
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and actions taken within AdSync Pro
  • Payment information — billing details processed securely via Paystack (we never store your card number)
02

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the AdSync Pro platform
  • Generate AI-powered ad copy tailored to your products
  • Create and manage ad campaigns on your behalf across connected platforms
  • Send transactional emails such as account confirmations and billing receipts
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your data is used solely to provide and improve our services.

03

Third-Party Integrations

AdSync Pro connects to third-party platforms on your behalf. When you authorize a connection, we access only the permissions you grant:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — ads management, ad account reading, and business management to create and track campaigns
  • Shopify — read access to your products, orders, analytics, and inventory to power ad creation
  • Takealot — your seller API key is encrypted and stored to sync product data
  • TikTok — ad campaign management and catalog syncing via TikTok Business API
  • Paystack — handles payment processing; we receive transaction confirmation but never your card details

Each integration is governed by its own platform's privacy policy. You can disconnect any integration at any time from your AdSync Pro dashboard.

04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted on Railway (PostgreSQL). We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Sensitive tokens (API keys, OAuth tokens) are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text
  • Access to production databases is restricted to authorised personnel only

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your AdSync Pro account.

05

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Disconnection — revoke access to any third-party platform at any time via your dashboard
  • POPIA Compliance — AdSync Pro complies with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). You may contact our Information Officer at admin@adsyncpro.com

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@adsyncpro.com.

06

Cookies

AdSync Pro uses minimal cookies and browser storage to keep you logged in (via JWT tokens stored in localStorage). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not share cookie data with third parties.

07

Children's Privacy

AdSync Pro is a business platform intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.
