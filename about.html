Who We Are

AdSync Pro is a South African SaaS platform developed by Infinite Select (Pty) Ltd, a technology company focused on e-commerce automation tools for the African market.

We help small and medium online retailers compete through smarter advertising automation — connecting their stores to TikTok, Meta, and Google Ads from a single, unified dashboard.

Founded in 2023 and headquartered in Midrand, Johannesburg, we understand the unique challenges of selling online in South Africa — from Takealot marketplace dynamics to ZAR billing and local logistics.
